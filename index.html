Details and Tracks

Environment
Develop products or tools that can be used to boost environmental efforts.

Carbon tracking tools, for example, or industry offset calculators are good projects in this field.
Some good tools include the Carbon Interface API along with the AirVisual Pollution API to give you some data to work with.
Education
Build a product that will accelerate learning, either in the classroom or at home.

Using AI to optimize learning systems might be a good idea.
As students yourselves, think of problems that you face in your educational experience, and how the use of technology can help solve them.
Social Justice
Create a product that can tackle social justice issues in the modern world.

Solving societal issues and inequalities is a great use for technology.
Think about building something that can help spread awareness, or help people in need. In the bay, we are surrounded by these issues, so think about how you can help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How large can a team be?
Teams can be 1-4 people.
Where will Bellhacks be?
Bellhacks will be in the Lokey building at Bellarmine College Prep. It's the building that looks like a big L.
If I don't have a team, what do I do?
If you don't have a team before the event, but want to work with others, we will help form teams on the day of the event. Just bring yourself and we will arrange teams with who we have. It is also perfectly fine to work alone if you prefer going solo.
Is there anything specific we have to develop?
No, you can develop whatever your creativity allows you to! We have specific tracks for inspiration, but competitors are not restricted to those tracks.
